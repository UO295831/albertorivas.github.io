About Me

I am a Data Science & Engineering student at the University of Oviedo, specialising in MLOps and the end-to-end lifecycle of machine learning systems — from data ingestion and feature engineering to model deployment and continuous monitoring in production environments.

My academic journey was enriched by an Erasmus exchange at Sapienza University of Rome, where I deepened my expertise in cybersecurity and advanced computer science, developing a multidisciplinary perspective that I now apply to building resilient, secure data systems.
